# summary/

Human-readable Chinese-language summaries of knowledge base entries.

## Rules

- **Language**: Chinese (中文)
- **Tone**: Storytelling (讲故事), may be humorous
- **Length**: 5–15 sentences
- **Naming**: 1:1 mapping with parent document (same filename)
- **Goal**: A reader who reads only the summary can say: "我知道这个文件在讲什么了。"

## Content requirements

- What knowledge is being captured
- What is the practical insight or pattern
- Why it matters to the team
- One sentence on what could go wrong without this knowledge

## What summaries are NOT

- Not translations of the full document
- Not technical specifications
- Not approval-gated (anyone can write or update them)

## Example

Parent: `docs/knowledge/<domain>/knowledge-entry.md`
Summary: `docs/knowledge/summary/knowledge-entry.md`
